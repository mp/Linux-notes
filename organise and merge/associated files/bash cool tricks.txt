BASH TRICKS (application independent)
-------------------------------------

AND, OR

cd [folder] && ls || echo "folder does not exist"


MULTIPLE TERMS

ls *.{clean, records}


NEGATIVE TERMS

shopt -s extglob
ls !(*.dat)	# anything that is not .dat

for in list:
for folder in A B
do
	ls $folder
done
